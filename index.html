FAQs

So, who does what?

  • Has Travelgate-X become a consolidator?
    Absolutely not. We are creating a new way to serve our clients, just like we once built the largest hub of suppliers in the world, we are now building a hub of direct connect to hotels.
  • Is Travelgate-X acting as principal?
    Absolutely not, Travelgate-X continues acting purely as a tech partner. Hotels are fully responsible for the delivery of service and operations at all times.
  • What is the role of the sales channels then?
    The sales channels is the key partner of the hotel on the distribution of its inventory.
  • Is Travelgate-X acting as a channel manager?
    No, HotelDirect-X has access to the hotel rates and availability of +50 connections with channel managers where hotels have their repository of product.
  • Do I still have to sign an agreement with hotels?
    There is no need to sign agreements with every single hotel or chain. The contractual relationship between the sales channels and the hotel is born simultaneously and tacitly with the formalization of the reservation through the HotelDirect-X service.
  • Do I still have to sign an agreement with channel managers?
    There is no need to sign agreements with channel managers. Travelgate-X will take care of the connectivity with hotels wherever hotels keep their inventory updated on.

What’s is it for me?

  • What’s in my commission?
    The sales channels will receive public dynamic rates additionally to the commission negotiated with the hotel. This could vary from hotel to hotel at every moment.
  • How can I activate HotelDirect -X?
    Easy, request your free activation as usual by filling in the form on the following link https://www.travelgatex.com/connect/form/legacy. Remember, this connectivity is free of charge!
  • Is there any minimum business required?
    No, you can start buying from one booking. No restrictions at all.

Costs

  • Is there any connectivity cost?
    There are no hidden costs in activating, DirectX. For all effects and purposes, DirectX would work just like another Premium Partner. No setup, no maintenance, no usage cost. It is the Hotels who are covering it in order to get direct access to you!

Product

  • What if I already buying the same hotel through a 3rd party supplier?
    Not a problem at all. DirectX will constitute an additional source of product at a potential different price and availability.
  • What hotels are going to be available on HotelDirect-X?
    We keep constantly expanding the portfolio of hotels available through HotelDirect-X. You can see the list of hotels we have included at any time via this link https://docsend.com/view/bsd8k5n
  • Is there any cache between systems?
    There is no cache system between the sales channels and the hotels. Travelgate-X can implement a cache solution if necessary, in order to improve the response time.
  • What about the mapping?
    You can either use our own mapping files or HotelDirect-X can provide you the full mapped product.
  • Do I have to take care of any systems setup?
    Not at all, it will just be one additional Premium connection via Travelgate-X.
  • What if, at some point, I want to sign a direct agreement with a hotel chain?
    No problem!. In that case, hotels would be also available through a traditional Travelgate-X connection instead of via HotelDirect-X.

Payments

  • Is it a prepaid or postpaid type of product?
    This is all about prepaid product meaning bookings should always be paid before the arrival of the final customer. This way of working should ensure the best rates and availability from hotels.
  • How much should I pay to the hotel?
    The sales channels will collect the commissionable rate from its customers. Once sales channels commission is deducted, they will proceed to pay the net rate to the hotel.
  • When should I pay?
    Full payment is due at the time of the booking in case of non-refundable rates. For refundable bookings, the sales channels will be responsible for settling payment before the booking enters the cancellation period.
  • What happen with cancelled bookings?
    Bookings must be cancelled online via Travelgate-X.
  • Should I pay Travelgate-X or should I pay the hotels directly?
    The Distribution Channel will only have to pay the net price to the hotel. No payment is required to Travelgate-X.
  • What are the payment types available?
    Prepayment is the key to get the most competitive rates and availability from the hotels. Travelgate-X has two alternatives at your disposal: Virtual Credit Card and Instant Transfers. In both cases:
    - Sales channels will have to sign an agreement with the financial entity and open an account.
    - Sales channels will have to allocate in advance the necessary amount of money.
    - Travelgate-X will double check at the time of the booking and before confirming it, that the necessary funds are available in the sales channels account. Otherwise bookings cannot be confirmed.
    - Travelgate-X will work with the most relevant financial companies providing both solutions.

Invoicing

  • What invoice should I expect to receive?
    Hotels will create an invoice and will send it to the sales channels for the net amount that the sales channels pay. HotelDirect-X will shortly be able to provide partners with its electronic billing service at no cost.
  • Any other invoice?
    No, that’s it. Hotels are covering HotelDirect-X tech fees.

Prices

  • Will I get commissionable or net rates?
    Travelgate will make sure the sales channels receives both the net and the commissionable rate for the same hotel room.
  • Can I add a markup on top of the rates delivered by hotels?
    Hotels will expect sales channels to sell at least at commissionable rates that are available on HotelDirect-X.
  • What content (texts and pictures) I will be receiving?
    The Distribution Channel will receive whatever is available in the channel manager, CRS or PMS that TravelageX is pulling the information from.
  • How will I receive remarks from hotels?
    Special remarks from hotels are provided by hotels in a text format and will arrive to the Distribution Channel through their API with Travelgate-X.

Content

  • What content (texts and pictures) will I be receiving?
    The sales channels will receive whatever content the channel manager, CRS or PMS has made available to Travelgate-X via a pull connection.
  • What if I need more content?
    Travelgate-X has signed an agreement with the content provider Icerportal.com ensuring you can have access to all its multilingual inventory of text and images at no cost.
  • How will I receive remarks from hotels?
    Special remarks from hotels are provided by hotels in a text format and will arrive to the sales channels through their API with Travelgate-X.
